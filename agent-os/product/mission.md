# Product Mission

## Pitch
Mike Lacey Portfolio is a professional portfolio website that helps industry professionals (producers, networks, and production companies) discover and evaluate Mike Lacey's extensive career as a Television Director by providing a compelling showcase of decades of work in sports and entertainment production.

## Users

### Primary Customers
- **Production Companies**: Looking to hire experienced directors for live broadcasts
- **Networks & Studios**: Seeking proven talent for sports, entertainment, and live events
- **Producers**: Evaluating directors for upcoming projects

### User Personas
**Industry Producer** (35-55)
- **Role:** Executive Producer or Line Producer
- **Context:** Staffing live sports or entertainment productions
- **Pain Points:** Difficulty finding experienced directors with verifiable credits; need to quickly assess fit
- **Goals:** Find a reliable, experienced director with proven track record in live production

## The Problem

### Discovering Qualified Directors
Finding experienced television directors with verifiable credentials and extensive live production experience is challenging. Production companies need to quickly evaluate a director's experience, credits, and professional reputation.

**Our Solution:** A comprehensive, professionally-designed portfolio that showcases Mike Lacey's career through credits, photos, testimonials, and biographical timeline - making it easy for industry professionals to evaluate his qualifications.

## Differentiators

### Decades of Verifiable Experience
Unlike newer directors with limited portfolios, Mike Lacey brings decades of experience across major networks (Golf Channel, Nickelodeon, Disney Channel, MTV, CBS Cable) with documented credits and testimonials.
This results in confidence for production companies when hiring for high-stakes live broadcasts.

### Comprehensive Career Documentation
Unlike simple resume sites, this portfolio includes photos from productions, video interviews, testimonial documents ("attaboys"), and a detailed career timeline.
This provides rich context that helps industry professionals understand the depth and breadth of experience.

## Key Features

### Core Features
- **Career Timeline**: Visual journey through decades in the industry, showing growth from shipping department to director's chair
- **Production Credits**: Comprehensive list of major productions across sports, entertainment, and live events
- **Photo Gallery**: Behind-the-scenes photos showcasing work with celebrities and major productions

### Professional Features
- **Testimonial Documents**: Scanned letters of appreciation from productions (attaboys)
- **Video Interviews**: Embedded YouTube interviews discussing career and approach
- **Professional Links**: Direct access to IMDb, LinkedIn, and contact information

### Contact Features
- **Contact Form**: Easy way for production companies to reach out
- **QR Code vCard**: Quick mobile contact exchange
- **Direct Contact**: Phone and email prominently displayed
